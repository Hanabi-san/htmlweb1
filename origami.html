Origami

Origami is a japanese art form that is well known all over the world. My interest in origami simply started on a whim when I wanted to learn to make the paper crane or "Orizuru" as it is called. I'm still learning but I greatly enjoy seeing complex patterns achieved through simple and neat folding. I look forward to being able to do many designs in the future.
