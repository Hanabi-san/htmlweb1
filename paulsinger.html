Paul Singer

When I first heard about Paul Singer, all i knew was that he was tenacious. Turns out I did'nt need to know too much else about him. He was very VERY tenacious. He became an interest of mine when I saw him take on gargantuan tasks and be sure of his own success. I've never seen anything like it and its fascinating. I want to one day be able to take on all kinds of challenges with the kind of self assuredness and skill Mr. Paul Singer has.
